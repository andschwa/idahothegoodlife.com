Who is Jack Schwartzmeyer?

I was born and raised in Southern California where I enjoyed a wonderful childhood as the youngest of six children. In 1984, I met and married my wife Amy, and six years later we moved to Coeur d'Alene, Idaho in search of a safe, wonderful community to raise our growing family.

There is never a dull moment in our home! We have five children of our own currently, and have enjoyed opening our home to numerous foster children over the years. As well as raising our family, Amy also teaches the Safe Sitter program at Kootenai Medical Center.

What sort of experience does Jack Schwartzmeyer have?

I started selling real estate in 2000 after several successful home purchases and soon realized that my passion is to help families find their perfect home and encourage them to make the very best investment of their life. I am licensed to sell real estate in both California and Idaho, and I hold a C-33 contractor’s license.

I am an active Rotarian and a member of the Post Falls Community Volunteers. I am a recipient of both the 2007 and 2008 Executive Club of Remax award and I was also named a member of the 2007 and 2008 Remax One Hundred Percent Club.
